Nairobi: The Green City In The Sun

Nairobi is the capital city of Kenya. The city lies in the south-central part of Kenya at an altitude of 1,795 meters. The city is the largest in Kenya and one of the largest in East Africa, being home to 4.3 million.

Nairobi is situated adjacent to the eastern edge of the Rift Valley. The most prominent geographical feature within the city are Ngong Hills, which are located west of the city. South of the city lies 113kmÂ² of plains, cliffs and forests that make up the famous Nairobi National Park. Scattered throughout the city are various parks and urban forests.

Nairobi National Park

Nairobi National Park is a national park that lies 7km south of Nairobi CBD. The park provides a unique opportunity where you can enjoy a safari while enjoying a backdrop of skyscrapers.
